A radical socialist libertarian pacifist futurist party, dedicated to the peaceful exploration of space in the name of all humankind.

So far The Space Party mostly exists as the members of a project on CoActivate.org. The coactivate project has 6 members, a wiki, and its own blog, only sporadically active.
